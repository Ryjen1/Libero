How It Works

From kickoff
to settlement.

01

Create a Match Room

Open Libero. Your device becomes a node. Share the topic with other fans — they connect peer-to-peer via Hyperswarm.

02

Log Events Live

Goals, cards, substitutions — one fan logs them, every connected device receives them instantly. No server required.

03

AI Analyzes in Real-Time

The local LLM reads the event feed and generates tactical insights. Formation analysis, win probability, tactical recommendations — all on-device.

04

Predictions Auto-Settle

Fans stake on outcomes. When the P2P feed confirms an event, the AI detects it and the wallet auto-settles. Winner gets paid. No house. No counterparty risk.
